Statics & Information

The educational purpose of the Department of Information Statistics is to cultivate data analysis experts who can make objective and scientific decisions by organizing, summarizing, organizing, analyzing, and knowledge complex and diverse data generated in modern society based on statistical theory and various statistical methodologies. In addition, it is to train information system development and managers with programming skills for information system development and ability to manage information systems and information and communication networks.

Coesmetics

This major aims to cultivate integrated cosmetics professionals who combine creative thinking and professional practical skills at the same time. To that end, students can have active and creative thinking skills by applying emotional value to academic basic concepts such as chemistry, chemical engineering, and life science to pursue systematic education that combines a wide range of knowledge from cosmetics planning, marketing, raw material analysis, and cosmetics development and manufacturing. It also focuses on cultivating practical skills that can be applied directly in the field after graduation by establishing a joint cooperation network with the domestic cosmetics industry to activate human exchanges and information exchanges, and providing students with specialized educational environments such as field training, internships, and self-directed competencies.
